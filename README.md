I weave computational logic with historical and creative narratives. By integrating machine learning with the humanities, I build systems that don't just process data, but understand context. I prefer the pragmatic, observational humor of Sir Dinadan over blind quests, and I bring that same grounded efficiency to my technical research.

### ✧ Current Pursuits
* **Algorithmic Intelligence:** Architecting path/policy search algorithms and PID drone controls (MSc Computer Science, Georgia Tech).
* **Machine Vision & Art:** Decoding the Gentileschi attribution debate through computational analysis (MA Art History, Ben-Gurion University).
* **Celtic & Arthurian Studies:** Exploring the nuances of medieval humor and narrative structure (MA Celtic Studies, UWTSD).
* **Creative Writing:** Drafting time-travel fiction steeped in "destined legend" tropes and the atmospheric magic of Edinburgh.

### ✧ Featured Architecture
* **[The Gentileschi Project](https://github.com/ladyFaye1998):** A machine-eyes approach to classical art history attribution.
* **[Ded Moroz Escape](https://github.com/ladyFaye1998):** A dual-language mobile AR escape room environment.
* **Automated Search Strategy:** Algorithmic search optimization (BSc CS, University of London).
