I research AI. I research *with* AI. I research alongside it and, occasionally, with great pleasure, completely without it.

I make art and study it. I build games from the bones of history and literature. I write. Somewhere at the intersection of machine learning, brushstroke analysis, Celtic mythology, and market logic, there is a pattern. I am here to find it — and then build a world around it.

Off-screen: horseback, armor, or both.
